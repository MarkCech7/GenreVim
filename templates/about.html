About

GenreVim is a web application designed to provide music genre classification for music videos from YouTube. Leveraging the power of DistilHuBERT, GenreVim analyzes audio features and patterns to identify the genre of a given music video.
Features
  • Accurate Genre Classification
    GenreVim uses sophisticated HuBERT algorithm to classify music genres accurately.
  • Easy-to-Use Interface
    The user-friendly interface makes it simple for anyone to use. Just enter the YouTube URL of the music video, and GenreVim will do the rest.
  • Recent and Recommended Songs
    GenreVim not only provides genre classification but also offers a curated list of recent and recommended songs based genre.
Supported Genres
GenreVim supports up to 18 genres including:
  • Blues
  • Classical music
  • Country music
  • Drum & Bass
  • Dubstep
  • Folk
  • Future Bass
  • Hardstyle
  • House
  • Jazz
  • Latin
  • Metal
  • Pop
  • R&B, Soul
  • Rap
  • Reagge
  • Rock
  • Trap
